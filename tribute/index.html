TIMELINE

  • Born in Tokyo on January 5, 1941
  • In April 1963, Miyazaki started working at Toei Animation, working on Watchdog Bow Wow and Wolf Boy Ken
  • In 1968, Miyazaki worked as chief animator, concept artist, and scene designer on Hols: Prince of the Sun
  • In August 1971, Miyazaki left Toei Animation to A Pro
  • In 1971, Miyazaki co-directed 14 eps of the first Lupin 3
  • In 1973, Miyazaki worked at Nippon Animation
  • In 1978, Miyazaki directed his first TV series, Conan, The Boy in Future
  • In 1979, Miyazaki moved to Tokyo Movie Shinsha to direct his first movie, the classic Lupin III: The Castle of Cagliostro.
